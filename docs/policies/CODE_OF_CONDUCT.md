# Code of Conduct

## Purpose
Establish community norms and expected conduct for boilerhaus-ops contributors.

## Our Commitment
As contributors and maintainers of this project, we pledge to make participation
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background.

## Expected Behavior
- Use inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what benefits the organization

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Sharing confidential information without authorization
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission

## Reporting
Contact the project owner privately if you observe or experience unacceptable
behavior. All reports will be reviewed and investigated.

## Enforcement
Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in removal from the project.

## Scope
This applies to all boilerhaus-ops repositories and related community spaces.
