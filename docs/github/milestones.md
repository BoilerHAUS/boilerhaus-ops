# Boilerhaus Milestones

Use this file as the active milestone map for Boilerhaus launch work.

## 1. Company formation

Goal:
Create the legal company cleanly and make the naming path explicit.

Work:

- legal name and fallback decision
- federal incorporation filing
- directors, officers, shares, and ISC setup
- Ontario business-name and operating registrations as needed

## 2. Finance and compliance

Goal:
Make Boilerhaus able to take money, track money, and stay compliant.

Work:

- CRA access
- GST HST decision
- payroll decision
- business banking
- bookkeeping
- invoicing and proposal flow

## 3. Operating system

Goal:
Stand up the minimum internal systems needed to run the company without chaos.

Work:

- repo docs
- Nextcloud or file structure
- email and admin ownership
- password manager and 2FA controls
- legal and finance record storage

## 4. Go-to-market foundation

Goal:
Make the first commercial motion sellable and trackable.

Work:

- founder-led service offer
- pricing hypotheses
- discovery and demo flow
- lead tracker and target account list
- website and contact basics

## 5. Launch readiness and cadence

Goal:
Operate the business on a weekly rhythm and close launch blockers deliberately.

Work:

- weekly metrics review
- launch readiness review
- blocker tracking
- first recurring operating cadence
