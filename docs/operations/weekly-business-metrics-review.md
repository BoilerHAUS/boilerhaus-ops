# Boilerhaus Weekly Business Metrics Review

## Purpose

Define the weekly scorecard for Boilerhaus while launching `ScopeHouse`.

This review exists to answer one question:

Are we learning fast enough and removing launch blockers fast enough to turn Boilerhaus into a real operating business?

## Rules

1. Track metrics that change decisions.
2. Keep setup blockers visible until they are closed.
3. Review the same core fields weekly.
4. Tie every section to next actions.
5. Separate real signal from busywork.

## Review cadence

Run this once per week.

Suggested structure:

- 15 minutes, launch blockers and company setup
- 15 minutes, pipeline and outreach
- 15 minutes, validation and sales
- 15 minutes, revenue and product-linked usage
- 15 minutes, decisions and assignments

## Weekly scorecard

### 1. Launch blocker metrics

Track:

- open `P0` issues
- open legal or compliance blockers
- open finance blockers
- blockers closed this week
- overdue critical tasks

Questions to ask:

- What is still blocking the company from operating cleanly?
- Which blocker has stayed open too long?
- What can be closed this week instead of discussed again?

## 2. Pipeline metrics

Track:

- new leads added
- total active leads
- outreach sent
- replies received
- discovery calls booked
- discovery calls completed

Questions to ask:

- Are enough new leads entering the pipeline?
- Is outreach turning into real conversations?
- Are weak-fit leads consuming too much time?

## 3. Validation and sales metrics

Track:

- customer interviews completed
- strong pain signals found
- repeated objections
- service conversations
- proposals sent
- deals closed

Questions to ask:

- Which pain points repeat most?
- Which objections are increasing?
- Where is the commercial motion breaking?

## 4. Revenue metrics

Track:

- revenue booked this week
- cash collected this week
- unpaid invoices
- pipeline value of open proposals

Questions to ask:

- Is money actually landing or only being discussed?
- Are follow-ups needed on open proposals or invoices?
- Which offer closes fastest?

## 5. Product-linked usage metrics

Track:

- active design partners
- new projects created
- intake completed
- scope drafts completed
- exports generated
- returning users

Questions to ask:

- Are users reaching the core planning loop?
- Where are users getting stuck?
- Which setup step needs more support?

## 6. Operating discipline metrics

Track:

- active leads with next action set
- overdue follow-ups
- tracker rows updated this week
- docs or decisions updated this week
- issues created or closed this week

Questions to ask:

- Are leads going stale?
- Is the operating system staying current?
- Are we turning learning into tracked work?

## Minimum weekly dashboard
